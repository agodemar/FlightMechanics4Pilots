Course Home

This material introduces aircraft flight mechanics and performance in an accessible manner. It covers all the inherent concepts in simple terms and relies on purely physical principles. The important physical laws underlying the study of airplanes moving through the air are explained by means of simple examples and illustrations. The main goal of this effort is providing an introductory overview of the mechanics of flight that conforms with the typical background of military pilots.

A basic but solid knowledge in physics and mathematics is required, although the peculiar concepts of flight physics will be presented with the due consideration of pilots attitude to the subject matter.
